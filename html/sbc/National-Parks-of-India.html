National Parks of India
Important Note :
  • Area established under section-35(4) of the Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972.
  • IUCN : International Union for Conservation of Nature .
  • Total no. of National Parks in India : 106 according to National Wildlife Database , Jan 2023.
  • First National Park in India :
    • Hailey National Park - established in 1936.
    • Now known as Jim Corbett National Park.
  • Largest National Park in India :
    • Hemis National Park(Ladakh) - 4400 km2
    • Also Largest National Park in South Asia.
    • High Altitude National Park.
    • Snow Leopard is the main attraction.
  • Smallest National Park in India :
    • South Button Island National Park(Andaman and Nicobar Islands) - 0.03 km2
  • States with largest number of National Park : Madhya Pradesh - 11 National Parks. [Latest addition - Kuno National Park]
  • First Marine National Parks in India :
    • Marine National Park - Gulf of kutch (Gujarat).
    • Total 6 Marine National Parks in India.
  • Floating National Park in the world : Keibul Lamjao National Park,Manipur (Loktak Lake)
